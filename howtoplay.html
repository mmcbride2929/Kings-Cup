How To Play

  • Select the amount of players participating.
  • Players take turns drawing cards from the deck. Every card drawn has a unique challenge associated with it.
  • Once the challenge is over, the loser must take a drink, and the next player draws a card.
  • Eventually the virtual can tab will pop.. whoevers turn it is when the tab breaks must finish their drink!
  • Have fun, & Drink Responsibly
